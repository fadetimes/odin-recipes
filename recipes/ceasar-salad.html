Ceasar Salad

Back to Recipes Ceasar Salad Image

Ingredients

This is a recipe for a classic Caesar salad!

For the Caesar salad, you will need:

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce and Caesar dressing. Toss until the lettuce is well coated.
  2. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and croutons to the salad. Toss gently to combine.
  3. If using, add minced garlic and lemon juice for extra flavor. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
  5. Enjoy your fresh and delicious Caesar salad!
